FAQ's

  • About MLE

    1. What can MLE offer?
      Tutorial services that is fast, reliable, safe and effective all in the push of a button.
    2. What’s the difference between finding clients online, versus locally?
      Technology improves the connection between the relationship of clients and tutors by offering better quality services, faster response times, cheaper costs and more accessibility to the teaching market.
    3. How does MLE make money?
      MLE charges tutors up to 5-20% service fee based on the total amount billed with a client.
    4. What is the Top-Rated program?
      Our Top-Rated Program is designed to showcase talent who consistently deliver at the top level of their field.

    About Hiring

    1. What should I do after I register?
      Define your objective. What do you want to learn? Outline your goals, deliverables, the skills you're looking for, and your desired deadline.
    2. How do I make the most out of my learning experience?
      Start by writing a clear and concise post. Be specific with what you want and the timeline in which you want it to be accomplished.
    3. What if I’m not happy with the results?
      The hiring of tutors undergo a screening process to assure quality service so most should provide satisfactory results. However, should a tutor consistently provide lacklustre teaching, it will be reflected in their profiles and MLE will step in to offer help.
    4. How can I ensure that my profile information is safe?
      The Service Contract terms in our User Agreement state that you own any intellectual property that you pay for. Furthermore, MLEs Terms of Service contain default Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) which many clients consider sufficient for this purpose.
    5. How do I know my tutor is accurately billing for my project?
      You can evaluate your tutor's activity through hourly, daily, or weekly reports.
    6. How do I pay my tutor and what does it cost?
      You and your tutor agree upon the price per project scope and budget. MLE makes paying tutors simple and secure. Choose to pay using MasterCard, Visa, American Express, or PayPal. Any payments are subject to a 2.75% processing fee.
    7. How often will I be charged?
      Hourly tutorial services use our automated billing system. Fixed-price tutorial services are divided into agreed-upon milestones.
    8. Can I get an official invoice?
      Yes, all payments to tutors have invoices that can be downloaded or printed.
    9. Do I have to file tax forms for my freelancer?
      You should not be responsible for filing 1099-MISC forms for your tutor.
    10. What do I look for in a tutor that is right for me?
      Most clients look for tutor who have; 1. The skills to get your job done and 2. A track record of satisfying clients.
    11. How do I hire on MLE?
      After you sign up, find a tutor and click the “Hire” button to get started.

    12. Does MLE screen freelancers?
      We have several measures in place to ensure MLE is a fair and reliable marketplace. We use multiple means to verify that tutors are who they say they are. Information is also displayed that gives you a sense of each person’s skill level. In part, this includes:
      Authenticating email addresses
      Displaying each freelancer’s Job Success score and feedback on past projects
      Providing results of online skills tests
      Ultimately, it’s your responsibility to screen prospective tutors to make sure their skills and experience meet the needs of your project. Custom screening questions can be included in your job post, and MLE Messages provides chat and video conference tools for interviewing finalists.

    About Tutoring

    1. How do I get started tutoring through MLE?
      All you need to get started is a computer, Internet connection, and amazing skills.
    2. Who can find tutorial and teaching opportunities online?
      Anyone with professional skills and a reliable Internet connection can use MLE to find opportunities and build a thriving business.
    3. What’s the best way to work with a client?
      In many ways, working with a client is just like working with any other client one would find in the teaching industry.
    4. How can I find the right teaching post for me?
      MLEs matching and search capabilities help you pinpoint interesting job posts.
    5. How does MLEs platform help me manage my work?
      MLE provides a user-friendly platform to help you chat and share files, track your time, and get paid.
    6. Why kind of clients will I find on MLE?
      Clients using the MLE platform all belong to the city of Baguio and its surrounding affiliates. Some clients may even be from cities all over the Philippines. However, location and distance issues are to the discretion of the tutor and client.
    7. Why should I tutor through MLE?
      MLE gives you the freedom to work anytime, from anywhere as soon as you accept a job posting. You can quickly and easily grow your tutorial business with ongoing opportunities.
    8. How much can I earn?
      That’s up to you. On MLE, the tutors themselves set and negotiate their own rates.
    9. How much does MLE cost?
      It's free to join MLE. We receive a 20%, 10%, or 5% fee from each payment depending on the amount billed to the client.
