IF YOU'RE TUTORING

  1. Find rewarding clients
    1. Freedom to work on ideal projects.
      On MLE, you run your own business and choose your own clients. Just complete your profile and we’ll highlight ideal jobs. Also search for job postings, and respond to client invitations.
    2. Wide variety and high pay.
      Clients are now posting jobs in hundreds of skill categories, paying top price for great services.
    3. More and more success.
      The greater the success you have on projects, the more likely you are to get hired by clients that use MLE.
  2. Get hired quickly
    1. Streamlined hiring.
      MLE’s sophisticated algorithms highlight projects you’re a great fit for.
    2. Top Rated and Rising Talent programs.
      Enjoy higher visibility with the added status of prestigious programs.
    3. Do substantial work with top clients.
      MLE pricing encourages tutors to use MLE for repeat relationships with their clients.
  3. How do I get hired by clients?
    1. It all starts by creating a great profile and submitting a compelling job proposal.
      Getting tutor work is a lot like traditional searching — you submit applications on certain jobs, then impress potential clients before being offered the teaching service.
    2. Creating an outstanding tutor profile to showcase your business or yourself.
      Think of it as an introduction, resume, and marketing brochure rolled into one, highlighting your: professional skills, experience, and portfolio; education and accomplishments; and online skills test results. The best profiles are complete, well-written, error-free, and feature a professional, friendly-looking picture.
    3. Sending the client, a compelling proposal.
      This will greatly increase your likelihood of getting an interview, so consider making sure yours is: professional, well-written, and error-free; tailor-made for the work you're seeking; and a true representation of your strengths and skills.
  4. Get paid on time
    1. All invoices and payments happen through MLE. Count on a simple and streamlined process.
    2. Hourly and fixed-price projects.
      For hourly work, submit timesheets through MLE.
      For fixed-price jobs, set milestones and funds are released via MLE escrow features.
    3. Multiple payment options.
      Choose a payment method that works best for you, from direct deposit or PayPal to wire transfer and more.
